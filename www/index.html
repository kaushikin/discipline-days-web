Need Help Now?

If you feel an urge or feel like giving up, press this button.

Emergency Reset Plan

Pause. You do not need to act on this urge. Give yourself 10 minutes.

  1. Take 5 slow deep breaths.
  2. Drink a glass of water.
  3. Stand up and move away from your phone or trigger.
  4. Do 10 push-ups or walk for 2 minutes.
  5. Remind yourself: “I only need to win this moment.”
  6. Wait 10 minutes before making any decision.
  7. Message or talk to someone you trust if the urge is strong.

Quick Distractions

  • Go outside for fresh air
  • Take a cold shower or wash your face
  • Play music and clean your room
  • Write your trigger in the notes
  • Open your goals and remember why you started

You are not weak. This urge is temporary. Your discipline is permanent.
